Software implementations and analysis of three voting systems

by Adrian Salamon

I have tested three different voting systems and programmed them. On this website you can submit a digital vote that can be used by the programs i have created. In this hypothetical example, three candidates should be elected to represent Kungsholmen in the Swedish parliment. Each party has one candidate. Rank all your prefrences and vote wisely! Lower number means higher preference.
