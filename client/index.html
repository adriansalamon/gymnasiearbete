Software implementations and analysis of three voting systems

by Adrian Salamon

I have tested three different voting systems (methods for how to choose what candidates should be elected in an election) by programming them. I have tested three methods: First-past-the post, Single Transferrable Vote and the Schulze method.

On this website you can submit a digital vote that can be used by the programs i have created. In this hypothetical example, three candidates should be elected to represent Kungsholmen in the Swedish parliment. Each party has one candidate. Rank all your prefrences and vote wisely! Higher position on the list means higher preference.
